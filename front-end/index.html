FAQ

CNFT stands for Cardano Non-Fungible Token. This phrase describes a unique piece of non-interchangeable data (such as an image or video) that is stored on a digital ledger such as the Cardano blockchain.
Blockchain Samurai takes a non-traditional approach to the standard practices of other NFT projects. Through our intermittent release schedule, we will be implementing a feature that we have termed Community-Driven Development. Each season and/or chapter in the Blockchain Samurai project will contain opportunities for the CNFT holders to interact with each other and the developers to determine the future of the project. These events will determine the lore that develops between existing samurai and even the potential survival of your samurai clan. At the end of every season, the current Era will progress and new samurai will emerge as the next generation of the Blockchain Samurai.
Every Blockchain Samurai is independently hand-drawn by our team of digital artists. While many large NFT projects are randomly generated, we believe that taking this non-traditional approach will allow us to build an interactive world around our project that the community can participate in and grow with.
In total, 10,000 Blockchain Samurai will be available to mint throughout the lifetime of the project.
Blockchain Samurai will be released in flights of 100 samurai which are known as Chapters. Every 10 chapters will make up a Season of 1,000 samurai. Likewise, 10 seasons will encompass the entirety of Blockchain Samurai at this point in time. Our Season 1 launch date will be announced via social media, our development blog, and email subscriptions.
Each Blockchain Samurai CNFT from Season 1 will be available to mint for 50 ADA.
Any wallet that is compatible with Cardano NFTs is capable of minting a Blockchain Samurai. A few examples of these wallets could include Daedalus, Yoroi, Adalite, Nami etc. DO NOT send ADA from an exchange to mint a CNFT or you will not receive it and your ADA will be lost.
